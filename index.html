Salua Union Blood Donors Club

About Us

Salua Union Blood Donors Club is a community-driven initiative aimed at saving lives by connecting blood donors with those in need. Our mission is to create a reliable network of voluntary blood donors and raise awareness about the importance of blood donation.

Our Mission

To ensure a steady supply of blood for patients in need by encouraging voluntary blood donation and building a strong donor database.

Our Vision

To create a world where no one dies due to the unavailability of blood.
